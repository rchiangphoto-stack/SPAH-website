What to Expect at Your Dog's First Visit

Bringing your dog to a new vet can feel stressful — for both of you. At South Pasadena Animal Hospital, we make sure every first visit starts with a warm, calm welcome. Our front desk team will greet you and your dog by name, and we'll give your pup a moment to settle in before the exam begins.

Your veterinarian will perform a thorough nose-to-tail physical examination, checking your dog's weight, heart, lungs, teeth, skin, coat, eyes, and ears. We'll review any medical records you've brought, discuss your dog's diet, exercise routine, and behavior, and build a personalized care plan that fits your dog's needs and your budget. Whether you're bringing in a new puppy for their first round of vaccinations or a senior dog for a wellness checkup, every dog receives patient, unhurried attention.

Before you leave, we'll walk you through all of our recommendations, answer every question, and make sure you feel confident about the next steps for your dog's health.

Dog Vet Alhambra FAQ

How much does a dog wellness exam cost?

Our wellness exam fees are published on our pricing page so you can plan ahead. We believe in transparent pricing and will always discuss costs before recommending any treatment or additional diagnostics.

Do you offer dental cleanings for dogs?

Yes. We perform professional dental cleanings under safe anesthesia, including scaling, polishing, and extractions when needed. Dental disease affects over 80% of dogs by age three, so regular cleanings are an important part of your dog's overall health. Learn more on our dog dental page.

What dog vaccines does SPAH offer?

We offer all core vaccines for dogs including DHPP (distemper, hepatitis, parainfluenza, parvovirus) and rabies. We also provide lifestyle vaccines such as bordetella (kennel cough) and leptospirosis based on your dog's risk factors and lifestyle.

Do you provide senior dog care?

Absolutely. For senior dogs (typically age seven and older), we recommend more frequent wellness exams and comprehensive bloodwork panels to catch age-related conditions early. We monitor for arthritis, kidney disease, heart conditions, and other common senior dog health concerns.

How do I book an appointment for my dog?

You can book online in under a minute, or call us at (626) 441-1314 and our team will find a time that works for you. We recommend scheduling in advance to ensure prompt availability.

Can you help with my dog's skin allergies?

Yes. Skin allergies are extremely common in Southern California dogs. Our team regularly helps dogs dealing with itching, hot spots, ear infections, and other allergy-related symptoms. We work with you to identify triggers and build a management plan that keeps your dog comfortable year-round.
