Dedicated Care for Non-Traditional Pets

Exotic animals have unique physiology, dietary needs, and environmental requirements. We treat every species with the dedicated care they deserve.

🦎

Reptiles

Bearded dragons, leopard geckos, ball pythons, corn snakes, turtles, tortoises, and iguanas. We address husbandry-related illness, metabolic bone disease, respiratory infections, and more.

🦜

Birds

Parrots, cockatiels, conures, finches, and budgies. Wellness exams, wing and nail trims, beak trims available. Feather-plucking evaluation and nutritional counseling.

🐇

Rabbits

All breeds welcome. Comprehensive wellness exams, dental assessments (malocclusion is common), spay and neuter, GI stasis treatment, and ongoing preventive care.

🐹

Guinea Pigs & Hamsters

Comprehensive small mammal care including respiratory issues, dental problems, skin conditions, and vitamin C deficiency management for guinea pigs.

🐔

Chickens & Poultry

Backyard flock health checks, egg-binding treatment, respiratory infections, parasite management, and injury care for your hens and roosters.

🐾

Other Small Mammals

Ferrets, chinchillas, hedgehogs, rats, mice, and more. If you have an unusual pet, give us a call—chances are we can help.

Exotic Pet Services We Offer

Every exotic pet visit starts with a thorough review of your pet's husbandry—enclosure setup, temperature gradients, lighting, substrate, and diet. Many exotic pet health issues stem from subtle environmental problems, so getting the basics right is the foundation of good care.
