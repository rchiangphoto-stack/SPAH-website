About Us

Your neighbors in
Alhambra / South Pasadena.

We are a privately-owned, small animal hospital led by Dr. Sylvia Chiang and Dr. Gina Navia. We pride ourselves in creating a warm and welcoming environment where both you and your pets feel comfortable.

From preventive care and surgery to exotics, our broad and varied services can meet all of your pet's health needs in one place. Whether you're bringing your pet in for the first time, or have trusted us for years — we treat your animal as if it were our own.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care

Everything your pet needs, under one roof — with transparent pricing.

Wellness Exams

Annual and semi-annual check-ups to keep your pet healthy and catch issues early.

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's age, species, and risk factors.

Exotic Pet Care

Dedicated care for reptiles, birds, rabbits, guinea pigs, and other exotics. Now accepting new patients.

Diagnostics

In-house lab work, digital radiography, and advanced diagnostic tools for accurate results.

Dental Care

Professional cleanings and oral health assessments to prevent pain and disease.
