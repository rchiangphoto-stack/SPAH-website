What We Offer

Veterinary Services in Alhambra / South Pasadena

Comprehensive veterinary care for pets of all species, shapes, and sizes.

Preventive Care

Keeping your pet healthy before problems start

Wellness Exams

Annual and semi-annual physical exams to assess your pet's overall health, catch early signs of illness, and discuss preventive strategies.

Learn more →

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's species, age, and risk factors. We'll walk you through what's recommended and why.

Learn more →

Parasite Prevention

Flea, tick, heartworm, and intestinal parasite prevention and treatment. Year-round protection plans for dogs and cats.

Nutritional Counseling

Species-specific dietary guidance to support healthy weight, coat, and energy levels at every life stage.

Microchipping

Permanent ID for your pet — a quick, painless procedure that dramatically improves reunion chances if your pet ever gets lost.

Learn more →

Dental Cleanings

Professional dental scaling and polishing under anesthesia, plus home care recommendations for ongoing oral health.

Learn more →

Medical & Surgical

Advanced care when your pet needs it most

Diagnostics

In-house bloodwork, urinalysis, fecal testing, and digital radiography — fast results so we can start treatment without delay.

Soft Tissue Surgery

Routine and advanced surgical procedures performed with rigorous anesthetic monitoring and pain management protocols.

Spay & Neuter

Routine reproductive surgeries with modern anesthetic protocols and attentive post-operative care for a safe, smooth recovery.

Learn more →

Dermatology

Treatment for ear infections, skin conditions, allergies, hot spots, and chronic itching to keep your pet comfortable.

Dental Surgery

Tooth extractions, treatment of oral masses, and other dental procedures to relieve pain and restore your pet's quality of life.

Imaging

Digital X-rays to evaluate bones, organs, and internal structures — quick, low-radiation, and immediately available for review.

Laser Therapy

Non-invasive photobiomodulation therapy to reduce pain and inflammation and accelerate healing — used for arthritis, wounds, and post-surgical recovery.

Learn more →
