What to Expect at Your First Visit

Driving 15 minutes to try a new clinic is a commitment, and we want every minute of that drive to be worth it. When you and your pet arrive at SPAH, our front desk team will have your appointment ready, greet your pet by name, and make sure you both feel at ease before the exam begins.

Whether you're bringing a new puppy from the Charter Oak area, a senior cat from downtown Covina, or a cockatiel who's been acting lethargic, our team treats every animal with curiosity and care. How much does a vet visit cost for Covina pets?

Common Vet Services for Covina Pets

As a full-service animal hospital serving the Covina area, we provide care for pets at every stage of life. Annual wellness exams are the foundation — at each visit, our team performs a thorough physical, updates vaccinations, screens for early disease signs, and discusses diet, behavior, and lifestyle changes you've noticed at home. Preventive care almost always costs less and produces better outcomes than waiting for a problem to become serious.

Vaccinations are consistently one of the top reasons Covina families visit our clinic. We carry core vaccines for dogs and cats as well as lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's risk factors. Dental care is another major category: most dogs and cats show meaningful signs of periodontal disease before age three, and a professional cleaning can prevent painful tooth loss and systemic infection. Our services page details every procedure we offer.

Skin and allergy issues are extremely common in the San Gabriel Valley's dry climate, and we frequently see dogs and cats from Covina who are dealing with chronic itching, ear infections, and hot spots. Our team works with each family to identify triggers and build practical management plans for the long term. And for Covina residents with exotic pets, we're one of the few practices in the eastern SGV who can provide thorough wellness and sick-visit care for reptiles, birds, and small mammals. Whatever your pet needs, our team is ready.
