Common Vet Services for Monterey Park Pets

As a full-service animal hospital near Monterey Park, we see pets for a wide variety of needs throughout the year. Annual wellness exams are the foundation of preventive care, and we recommend them for pets of all ages. During these visits, our veterinary team performs a thorough nose-to-tail physical, updates vaccinations, and screens for common health concerns before they become serious.

Vaccinations are another top reason pet owners visit our clinic. We offer core vaccines for dogs and cats as well as lifestyle-based vaccines tailored to your pet's risk factors. Dental cleanings are one of the most requested services at our practice. Many pet owners are surprised to learn that dental disease affects the majority of dogs and cats over age three, and a professional cleaning under anesthesia can prevent painful infections and tooth loss down the road.

Skin allergies are extremely common in Southern California pets, and our team frequently helps dogs and cats from Monterey Park who are dealing with itching, hot spots, and ear infections related to environmental or food allergies. We work with each family to identify triggers and build a management plan that keeps their pet comfortable year-round. We also welcome exotic pets including rabbits, guinea pigs, reptiles, and birds for wellness checkups and sick visits, making us one of the few veterinary clinics near Monterey Park equipped to care for non-traditional companions.
