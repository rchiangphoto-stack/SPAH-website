Why Us

Why South Pasadena Families Choose SPAH

Steps from South Pasadena

Our address — 3116 W Main St — sits right at the city border where Alhambra ends and South Pasadena begins. Most South Pasadena residents arrive at our door in under five minutes, whether they're coming from Monterey Road, Fremont Avenue, or Fair Oaks.

Named After Your Community

South Pasadena Animal Hospital — the name says it all. When Dr. Chiang opened these doors, she intentionally chose a name that reflects the community this practice was built to serve. We're not a franchise parachuted into the neighborhood; we're your neighbors on Main Street.

Beyond Dogs & Cats

Finding a vet who confidently handles exotic pets in the South Pasadena area is tough. We're one of the few local practices with genuine experience in reptiles, birds, rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters, and other small mammals — so your bearded dragon gets the same quality of care as a Labrador.

Compassionate, Personal Care

We're a small, privately-owned practice — not a revolving door of unfamiliar faces. Dr. Chiang and Dr. Navia know your pet by name, remember their history, and take the time to answer every question. No corporate rush, no assembly-line appointments.
